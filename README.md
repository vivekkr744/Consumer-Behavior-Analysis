## 📌 Overview  
This repository contains a **Power BI dashboard and analysis report** on consumer behavior in the e-commerce sector for 2024. It highlights **purchase trends, customer demographics, payment preferences, and seasonal patterns** to help businesses make data-driven decisions.  

---

## 🚩 Problem Statement  
E-commerce businesses often face challenges in:  
- Identifying **top-performing product categories**.  
- Understanding **age groups contributing most revenue**.  
- Tracking **monthly purchase trends**.  
- Analyzing **payment methods and device usage**.  
- Recognizing **gender distribution in purchases**.  

---

## 🛠️ Methodology  
1. **Data Collection** – Purchase categories, amounts, age groups, payment methods, devices, and gender distribution.  
2. **Data Cleaning & Transformation** – Removed inconsistencies, standardized purchase categories, aggregated revenue.  
3. **Dashboard Creation (Power BI)** – Visualized purchase trends by category, age, month, payment mode, and device usage.  
4. **Analysis & Insights** – Identified top-performing categories, seasonal peaks, and customer preferences.  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

- **Top Categories**: Jewelry & Accessories, Sports & Outdoors, Electronics.  
- **Age Group Revenue**: Highest contribution from **24–29 years (57K)**.  
- **Seasonal Trends**: November–December peak due to holiday demand.  
- **Payment Methods**: Credit Card (21.01%) and Debit Card (19.15%) dominate.  
- **Device Usage**: Smartphones (35%) lead purchases.  
- **Gender Split**: Male (45.2%) and Female (44.9%) nearly equal.  

---

## ✅ Solutions & Recommendations  

- Focus marketing on **high-revenue categories**.  
- Target **24–41 age group** with personalized campaigns.  
- Boost **holiday season inventory and ads**.  
- Promote **digital wallets** and optimize for **mobile-first shopping**.  
- Ensure **inclusive marketing** for diverse gender identities.
